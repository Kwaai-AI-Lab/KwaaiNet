### q19 — What was the Non-European Unity Movement?

The movement's ten-point programme included demands such as the full and unfettered franchise (the right to elect and be elected to all councils of the state) and free and equal education to the age of sixteen [2]. The NEUM was notable for its emphasis on non-violence and non-collaboration with apartheid institutions, aiming to create a non-racial society through peaceful means.

The movement's ideas and methods were influential in shaping the consciousness of the epoch, contributing significantly to the struggle for freedom in South Africa.
