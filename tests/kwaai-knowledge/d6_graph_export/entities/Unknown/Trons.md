The Trons and the forces that held them together. It was all tremendously highbrow, but I think we “came out by the same door as in we went”. It was a bit of showing off about being “a varsity student”. We all proudly wore the yellow freshers’ tie and when it was time for it to be discarded in June we went about collecting signatures in blotty ink of all our friends, resolving I suppose to keep the tie as a memento forever and a day.
