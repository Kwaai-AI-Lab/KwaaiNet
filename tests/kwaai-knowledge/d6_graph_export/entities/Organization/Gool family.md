The Gool family is a prominent organisation in South Africa, operating primarily in Cape Town and Bombay. The family played a significant role in Indian politics and social activism, particularly during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. They were associated with key figures such as Mahatma Gandhi and were involved in lobbying efforts to counter proposed legislation.
