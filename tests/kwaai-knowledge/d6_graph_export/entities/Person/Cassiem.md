Cassiem was a South African butcher who owned a butchery in District Six during the era of apartheid. He was known for his jovial nature and ability to crack salacious jokes with his customers, mainly women. Cassiem's business was reeking of cooked sausage meat, and he had a pronounced swagger to his gait due to a hernia. His exploitative rent hikes on tenants in the tenement block, including the Rassools, would later come back to haunt him.
