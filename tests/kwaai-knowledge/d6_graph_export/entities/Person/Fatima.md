Fatima was a member of the D6 community, known for her association with various individuals and organizations in the area. She was particularly close to her cousin Yousuf (Joe) Rassool, who often stayed with her and her husband Dawood when he visited Durban. Fatima's home was a hub for social gatherings and cultural exchange, where she would serve traditional Natal Indian meals, including curried peanuts and chips. Her family played an important role in the community, with several members pursuing careers in medicine and education.
