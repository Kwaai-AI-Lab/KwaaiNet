Aunt Jane was a Cape Malay/Indian woman who lived in the early 20th century and made significant contributions to her community. She was one of the first Cape Malay/Indian girls to qualify as a Bachelor of Arts, achieving this partly at Fort Hare and then by private study in 1931. Her broad-minded vision and achievements were an inspiration to her family and community. Aunt Jane passed away on May 6th, 1996, at the age of 94.
