Solomon Tshekisho Plaatje was a South African man of his era, primarily known for being a visionary and a key figure in the fight for the rights of the black population during the early 20th century. He played a significant role in the formation of the South African Native National Congress and was a vocal opponent of the Land Act of 1913.
