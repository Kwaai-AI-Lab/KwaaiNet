Baboo Abed was a cricket player from South Africa in the mid-20th century who played for the Kismet CC and was known for his all-round skills, including bowling, batting, and keeping wicket. He was part of the Western Province Indian Cricket Union side that participated in the biennial cricket tournament in 1947.
