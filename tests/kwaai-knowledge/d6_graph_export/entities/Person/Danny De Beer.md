A member of the TLSA was Danny De Beer, the PE teacher, who had already done his degree through the University of South Africa, hence why we were able to share ideas about the courses offered there. I remember early in 1952 Danny and I had represented Chapel Street School at a meeting in the Star Cinema where an official of the Cape Education Department addressed the school delegates about the Van Riebeeck festival and the part the schools could play in celebrating the founding of the Cape Colony in 1652.
