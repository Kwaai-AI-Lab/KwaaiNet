Auntie Annie was a significant figure in the D6 community, known for her strong family bonds and traditional remedies. She played a crucial role in helping her relatives with their health issues, particularly when her cousin Naz fell ill with tuberculosis. Auntie Annie brought bottles of a mixture that had apparently cured Naz, which consisted of Dutch patent medicines guaranteed to cure Phthisis and spitting of blood. Her efforts were appreciated by the family, especially during difficult times.
