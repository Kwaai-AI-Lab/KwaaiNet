Patriarch of the Gool family; born in the Swat region of India, a spice merchant who migrated to South Africa in 1884 and settled in Cape Town. Built a 13-room mansion at 7 Buitencingle Street and established a mosque for the local Muslim community. Had two wives: Bibi (Peari Beghum) and Wahida Ta'al. Died April 1, 1940. Grandfather of Yousuf (Joe) Rassool, the memoir author.
