The Liberty Cavalcade was a funfair event that took place in an unspecified location, where people were distracted from their political awareness and participated in rides and attractions such as the whip, helter-skelter, coconut shying, darts, and seeing outlandish sights. The event was significant because it showed how people were not yet sufficiently politicised to abstain from distractions like the Liberty Cavalcade.
