A Kelvinator is a type of refrigerator, specifically a large humming tall white one purchased by the author's family during a time of relative affluence in the memoir 'LEST WE FORGET' by Yousuf (Joe) Rassool. Its significance lies in its appearance as a symbol of the family's improved economic situation.
