Big Bertha refers to a large-calibre howitzer used by the German army during World War I, but in this context, it is used metaphorically to describe a system of classification and categorization used by the Apartheid regime. This system was used to classify people into racial categories, with hundreds of White university and college students enrolled to process the population.
