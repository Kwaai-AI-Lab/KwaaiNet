The phrase 'It's just an old Shanty' is used to downplay the significance of traditional songs and folk music from a bygone era, often in the context of nostalgia or reminiscence. In the source text, it is used to describe the narrator's fond memories of singing old favourites with his aunt while helping her with household chores.
