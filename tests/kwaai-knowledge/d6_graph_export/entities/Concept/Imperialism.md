Imperialism refers to a policy or practice of extending the power and influence of one's own country or empire at the expense of another country or territory, often through military conquest, economic coercion, or cultural domination. In the context of the source text, imperialism is used as a concept to describe the conflict between the Western Bloc and the Eastern Bloc, with the forces of imperialism representing the capitalist West and the socialist camp representing the Soviet Bloc.
