Aunt's plight refers to a situation where an aunt is struggling financially due to societal restrictions and lack of opportunities for women in education and employment. In this context, it specifically describes the difficulties faced by married women who are no longer able to work as teachers due to regulations that prohibit them from continuing their careers after marriage.
