Annie Laurie refers to a traditional Scottish ballad, often sung in the late 19th and early 20th centuries, which expresses nostalgia for a lost love. In the source text, it is mentioned as one of the songs played by Yousuf's aunt while he helped with household chores, evoking memories of his own lost loved ones.
