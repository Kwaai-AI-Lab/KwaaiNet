The lesser evil is a concept that refers to the idea of accepting a less desirable option as the best available choice in a given situation. In the context of the source text, it is used by the government stooges who argue that having 'our man' inside promoting 'our point of view' is better than boycotting and potentially allowing someone else to be elected.
