Zionism refers to a political and ideological movement that aims to re-establish a Jewish homeland in Palestine, which was then under Ottoman rule and later became part of the British Mandate for Palestine. In the context of the source text, Zionism is significant as it is viewed by the author as unjustly displacing Arab inhabitants and leading to the creation of the State of Israel.
