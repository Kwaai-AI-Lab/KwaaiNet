Unity is Strength refers to a concept that emphasizes the importance of unity and solidarity in achieving strength and success. In the context of the source text, this concept is significant as it highlights the efforts of the architects of the Union of South Africa in 1910 to promote unity among different groups, including allowing the 'Non-White' parliamentary franchise in the Cape.
